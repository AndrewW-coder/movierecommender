// how it works
01
Tokenize
Movie genres are split into tokens. "Action|Thriller" → ["Action","Thriller"]
02
TF-IDF
Each token is scored by how rare it is. Common genres like Drama get lower weight.
03
Vectorize
Every movie becomes a point in high-dimensional genre space.
04
Cosine sim
The angle between two vectors determines similarity. Smaller angle = more alike.
05
Rank
Top 10 closest vectors are returned as your recommendations.
